🗺️ Leto 2026: The Ultimate Road Trip

14 Nights • 4 Countries • Family of 4 • Budget < 2000€

📅 Daily Itinerary
Days 1 - 4

🇮🇹 Lake Como & Lugano

Drive from Bratislava (9h). Explore Varenna, take ferries to Bellagio. Day trip to Lugano (CH) is only 30 min away.

🏠 Suggested Stay: Apartments in Canzo or Lecco area (~85-95€/night)
Days 5 - 8

🇮🇹 Lake Garda & Verona

Relax by the lake in Sirmione. Visit Gardaland for the kids. Day trip to the Arena di Verona (30 min drive).

🏠 Suggested Stay: Agriturismo near Peschiera del Garda (~90€/night)
Days 9 - 13

🇫🇷 French Riviera (Côte d'Azur)

Beach days in Menton and Nice. Afternoon in Monaco. Drive the scenic Great Corniche road.

🏠 Suggested Stay: Budget Apartment in Nice North or Menton (~110€/night)
Day 14 - 15

🇨🇭 Return via Swiss Alps

Drive through Gotthard Pass. Overnight in Lucerne. See the Chapel Bridge and enjoy lake views before the final leg home.

🏠 Suggested Stay: Hostel or Budget Studio in Lucerne (~140€)
