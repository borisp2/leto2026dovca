☀️ Leto 2026: Family Road Trip

Bratislava → Alps → Riviera → Switzerland

🇮🇹 Lake Como & Lugano

Base: Varenna (3 nights). Day trip to Lugano, Switzerland. Visit Villa Balbianello and Bellagio via ferry.

🇮🇹 Lake Garda

Base: Sirmione area (4 nights). Beaches, Gardaland, and a side trip to romantic Verona.

🇫🇷 French Riviera

Base: Nice (4 nights). Explore Monaco, Menton's sandy beaches, and the Promenade des Anglais.

🇨🇭 Swiss Alps Return

Stopover: Lucerne (1 night). Final lake views and mountain scenery before driving home.

Target Budget: < 2000€ (Travel & Stay)
